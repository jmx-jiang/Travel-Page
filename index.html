About

New York City has many tall buildings, including the Empite State Building, at 1,250 feet. It is a very busy city, and you can travel around it with the subway. You can find museums, including the Natural History Museum, right next to a subway station. New York City is also home to central park, containing 843 acres of land.

New York City is also the most populated city in the United States, with 8,335,897 people. It has double the population of Los Angeles. It is home to the headquarters of the United Nations, which makes this city important. There are also over 300,000 millionaires in New York City, as it is expensive to live in.
